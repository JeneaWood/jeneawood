Jenea Wood

ARTIST | GRAPHIC DESIGNER | EDUCATOR

Jenea Wood is a visual artist whose work stems from her practice of seeing beauty in the obvious. Using photography and painterly methods that include photo transfers onto found manmade and natural objects, she explores ways to reflect the simple beauty overlooked by habituation. Jenea completed her MFA in Graphic Design and Visual Experience from Savannah College of Art and Design, her BFA in Digital Art, and BA in Graphic Design from the University of West Florida. Jenea is currently living and teaching Graphic Design and Digital Art in Pensacola, FL.
